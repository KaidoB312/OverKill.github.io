About OverKill

OverKill is a lightweight, retro-neon FPS built entirely in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

It uses a classic raycasting technique to fake 3D walls, with simple sprite enemies, ammo drops, and multiple arenas. The goal is simple: clear each level as fast as you can without dying.

Designed to be readable, hackable, and fun to mod. Tweak the maps, enemy positions, or visuals and make it your own arena shooter.
